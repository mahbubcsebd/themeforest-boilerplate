You may need padding for your customized section. if you need padding top & bottom side of a section just add section, section-pb for padding bottom & section-pt for padding top to your class to add paading automatically

All you have to do is to just add them to your class if you face something like that. To minimize the padding from the top add pt-0 and also to minimize from the bottom add pb-0 and also if you want no margin or padding at all use m-0 & p-0
